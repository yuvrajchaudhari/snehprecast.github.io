About us

About Us

Sneh Precast is decade experienced organization manufacturing & erection of various precast elements like Paving blocks, U drain, Hume pipes, Compound wall, Kerb stone, Street furniture, RMC, DMM etc. with state of the art manufacturing setup at Pune. Sneh serves infrastructure, residential & industrial development of the country for applications like Highways, footpaths, industrial lands, storm/drainage water, petrol pump outlets, petrol pump outlets, Ports, cable trenches, Smart city etc. We are the largest manufacturer of blocks in Mahasrashtra with more than 2L blocks per day
Customer focus is our core value and we have been working tirelessly to deliver to the satisfaction of customers. PSUs like BPCL, IOCL, HPCL, corporates like L&T, Tata Projects, Reliance, MEIL, Mahindra, FIAT etc, Reputed builders like Kolte Patil, Lodha, Pride and many prestigious clients whom we are delivering to their project
We at Sneh have strong culture of value and we rigerousely follow them. We have strong growth plan and committed to deliver to the purpose.
